History

Mech.Me is a Philippine-based blog and forum for all things mechanical keyboard related where you will learn how to choose your personal keyboard and where to buy the parts you need to make it. In addition, build guides and community news will be updated regularly. Our goal is to provide a place where mechanical keyboard enthusiasts and beginners alike can discuss and learn from one another and share their own style of mechanical keyboards.
